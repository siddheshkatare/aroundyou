We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with third-party services strictly for functionality and analytics.

We implement appropriate measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee 100% security due to inherent risks in digital transmission.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, update, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at support@aroundyou.app.

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated via the app or email.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please email us at support@aroundyou.app.
